Editorial Design — 2024

Outline

Role Art Director
Duration 4 Months
Tools InDesign, Photoshop

Outline is a digital magazine exploring the intersection of design, culture, and technology. As Art Director, I was responsible for establishing the visual language that would define the publication's identity across its first twelve issues.

The editorial design challenge was creating layouts that feel fresh and contemporary while maintaining the readability and gravitas expected of long-form journalism. I developed a flexible grid system that adapts to different content types — from photo essays to data-driven features.

Typography played a central role in the design. The contrast between the bold display type and refined body copy creates a visual rhythm that guides readers through complex narratives without overwhelming them.
