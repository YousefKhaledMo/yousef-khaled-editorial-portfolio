Brand Identity — 2023

The Math Mentor

Role Brand Designer
Duration 2 Months
Tools Illustrator, Figma

The Math Mentor is an educational brand dedicated to making mathematics accessible and engaging for students of all ages. The challenge was creating a visual identity that feels approachable yet authoritative — something that says "math can be fun" without sacrificing credibility.

I developed a comprehensive brand system including logo, color palette, typography, and collateral design. The geometric logo represents the precision of mathematics while the warm, inviting colors create an environment where students feel comfortable learning.

The brand has been applied across business cards, social media templates, presentation decks, and a full website design — creating a cohesive experience that helps The Math Mentor stand out in the competitive tutoring market.
