UI/UX Design — 2024

TutorConnect

Role UI/UX Designer
Duration 3 Months
Tools Figma, Maze

TutorConnect is a platform designed to bridge the gap between students and qualified tutors. The challenge was creating an interface that feels both professional and approachable — balancing the trust required for educational services with the ease of use expected from modern apps.

The design process began with extensive user research, identifying pain points in existing tutoring platforms. I focused on creating a seamless booking flow, intuitive tutor profiles with verified credentials, and a messaging system that keeps communication organized.

Key design decisions included a warm color palette to reduce anxiety around learning, clear hierarchy in tutor listings to highlight qualifications, and a mobile-first approach since most users would access the platform on their phones between classes.
