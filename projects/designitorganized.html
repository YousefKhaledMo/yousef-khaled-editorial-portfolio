Web Design — 2023

DesignItOrganized

Role Web Designer
Duration 2 Months
Tools Figma, Webflow

DesignItOrganized is a resource platform for designers seeking to streamline their workflow and organize their creative process. The website needed to embody the very principles it teaches — clarity, structure, and intentional design.

I designed a clean, grid-based layout that uses whitespace as an active design element. The information architecture was carefully planned to help users find resources quickly, with a tagging system and search functionality that feels intuitive.

The responsive design ensures the experience translates seamlessly across devices, with thoughtful breakpoints that maintain the visual hierarchy whether viewed on a 27-inch monitor or a mobile phone. Custom micro-interactions add personality without distracting from the content.
